Crystal Collector

How to Play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystalyou will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number, you loose the game if your total score goes over the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
